Harvard University

A private university, regarded as the most prestigous university in the world by many. Located in Cambridge, Massachusetts this institution is part of the renowned Ivy League. It boasts an extremely high endowment and provides its student with a world-class education.

University of California, Berkeley

This university is part of the UC system, and is located in Berkeley, California. Being a public university, it caters to in-state students. Fun fact: Berkeley is the oldest university in the UC system.

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor

The University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, is a leading public research institution founded in 1817, renowned for its comprehensive academic offerings and top-tier research facilities. As the flagship campus of the University of Michigan system, it serves over 48,000 students.
